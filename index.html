**Llama-3[8B] Meditron V1.0** The first [*Llama-3*](https://llama.meta.com/llama3/) fine/instruct-tuning on biomedical data *by the Meditron Team @EPFL/Yale* Introducing Llama-3[8B]-MeditronV1.0---a new medical Large Language Model (LLM) with 8 billion parameters, fine-tuned within 24-hours of the release of Meta's Llama-3. This new model outperforms all state-of-the-art open models within its parameter class on standard benchmarks such as MedQA and MedMCQA. It also outperforms Llama-2[70B] and is within 10% of the current leading open model for medicine in the 70B range: Llama-2[70B]-Meditron. This work shows the innovative potential of open foundation models with widely available weights and is part of a larger initiative to ensure equitable participatory access to this technology in low-resource settings.
